Bitcoin can be bought and sold (traded) in a number of ways just like stocks, bonds and regular currency. Additionally, because it is a peer to peer currency, it can be traded amongst peers on peer to peer markets.

When you trade bitcoin, you are either buying, selling or holding it at any one time. It is important to look at the price and decide on the next step. Decide whether

  1. You want to make a profit by buying bitcoins when price is low and later, sell them at a higher price at a later date
  2. You want to profit by selling at high price and buying them back when price is low at a later date

Experienced traders may be willing to accept more risk for higher risk reward ratios.If you are one such trader, leverage trading bitcoin is an option Just like trading forex, bitcoin can be traded on margin though trading brokers.

For example SimpleFx.com and 1Broker.com accept deposits and withdrawals in bitcoin, and allow for normal and marginal trading 24/7.
